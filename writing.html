Essays

For my first example of writing that I have completed was a biography of my uncle Nou Toua Muas who was at the time 87. His life from Laos to America and his struggles through life. It is an assignment that I obtained from a teacher that made a big impact on how I viewed my own culture and my cultures origins. And the struggles that my elders took to arrive in America. This biography shows me how he went through his life from being a villager to a refugee to a citizen. His life changed when he lived in the refugee camp. I chose this sample to share because it is something that is important to me that has helped me gain my own confidence and my own thoughts and is helping me currently learn my purpose.
